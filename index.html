02. FAQ

What is the purpose of wPOKT?

As opposed to degenerative finance, regenerative finance is an implementation of Data Farming that creates a virtuous cycle because of its cause and effect relationship to the networks underlying utility.

In the case of wPOKT. The more wPOKT staked for developers, the more access to decentralized infrastructure they have, the more developer activity on the network, the more POKT rewards the staking pools receive, and the more liquidity between POKT<>wPOKT<>ETH the more responsive the ecosystem will be, and the more copyable this method is, the more decentralized the network will be.
